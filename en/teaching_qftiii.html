Quantum Field Theory III
Physics Master and Docrtoral and Post-Doctoral Studies programme
ETH Zürich

Winter 2010

Course content

The course contains a comprehensive introduction to supersymmetry in quantum field theories. The lectures cover most of the relevant theoretical aspects of the subject, as well as some more phenomenologically-oriented topics, including a presentation of the supersymmetric version of the Standard Model. The course includes 14 lessons with 2 hours main lesson plus 1 hour exercise session.
