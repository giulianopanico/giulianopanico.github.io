Hunting for Elementary Particles

This talk provides an introduction to basic topics in particle physics: The Scientific method, the Standard Model of particle physics and the Higgs boson, collider physics, open questions. It is address to 4th-and 5th-year high-shool students.

It was presented at Liceo Scientifico Stampacchia, March 26th 2018, Tricase (Lecce). (slides, in Italian)


Particle Theory at Collider

This talk provides an introduction to research topics in theoretical particle physics, with particular focus on collider phenomenology. It is addressed to university students.

It was presented in the SCIENCE lecture series organized by the Associazione Italiana Studenti di Fisica (AISF) at the University of Florence, December 9th 2019. (slides, in Italian)
